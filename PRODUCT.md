# Product

## Register

product

## Users

- **Owner/Manager**: runs the business, reviews performance, controls settings, needs fast answers and trustworthy numbers.
- **Accountant / Finance**: needs exports, reconciliation-friendly reports, VAT/tax views, and auditability.
- **Operators / Cashiers**: occasionally view simple operational reports; interface must be fast, obvious, and resilient.

Typical context: Windows desktop, in-store office or POS terminals, time-sensitive decisions, Arabic-first workflows.

## Product Purpose

ElHegazi Retailer is a desktop retail management system (POS + inventory + purchases + accounts + treasury + reporting).
Success means: reports are complete, accurate, fast to run, easy to filter, easy to export/print, and consistent across outputs.

## Brand Personality

Confident, practical, and precise.
Tone: direct Arabic UI, minimal fluff, clear labels, professional printouts.

## Anti-references

- Generic “SaaS dashboard template” look and repetitive card grids.
- Over-decorative effects (neon glows, gradient text, glassmorphism everywhere).
- UI that hides important filters behind confusing modals or requires too many clicks.

## Design Principles

1. **Arabic-first clarity**: RTL by default, consistent wording, and predictable layouts.
2. **Trust through consistency**: the same metric means the same thing in UI, PDF, Excel, and print.
3. **Fast paths for daily work**: common report flows should be one or two actions away.
4. **Progressive complexity**: show essentials first; advanced options are discoverable without clutter.
5. **Auditability**: every report/export/print can be traced, filtered, and reviewed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Maintain strong contrast for long sessions.
- Respect reduced motion where possible.
- Numeric/code fields must stay readable in RTL (LTR islands for SKU, references, invoice numbers).
